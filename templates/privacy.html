Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide Spendly's functionality — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts for typography, loaded directly from Google's servers. No other third-party services with access to your personal data are currently in use. If this changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided on our website.
